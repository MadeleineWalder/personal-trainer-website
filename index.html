About Me

Want to reach your fitness goals? I can help you. I can provide you with nutritional advice, diet plans, training programs and professional coaching sessions. I work individually, meaning that I personalise each experience based on your conditions, wishes and needs to achieve the best results. I am an ex-boxer with over 10 years of experience in bodybuilding, fitness and nutritional advice. I am also licensed as an international personal trainer and have EQF4 certification, which you can see below.

The basis for personal training is that it's customised to each individual's needs and conditions. It doesn't matter if you're a beginner in exercise and training, or if you're an elite athlete. I coach based on the principles of anatomy, physiology, sports science and nutrition. Together we can apply this knowledge in the best way possible based on your specific conditions. I have a supportive and motivating coaching style, meaning I will push you to have more energy, get better training routines and simply feel better!
